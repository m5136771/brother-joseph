Brother Joseph Discord Bot

About the Bot

Brother Joseph is a Discord bot created as a replacement for the now unsupported LDS-Bot. Its primary purpose is to provide text of scripture references from LDS-specific scriptures, currently focusing on the Book of Mormon. It is designed to support members of the LDS church and those interested in learning more. In the future, the bot will offer support for additional LDS publications and scriptures, as well as enhanced features such as searching and cross-referencing.
